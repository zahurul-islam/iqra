Privacy Policy

Iqra: Learn Quranic Words — Last Updated: March 2026

We don’t store any personal information except your email address.

1. Introduction

Iqra: Learn Quranic Words (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

Email Address Only

  • Required for account creation and authentication
  • Used for account recovery and important notifications
  • We do NOT collect names, phone numbers, or other personal details

Learning Progress Data

  • Words learned and chapter progress
  • Practice scores and streak information
  • Favorite words and preferences
  • This data is stored to provide a personalized learning experience

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email and learning data are used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync your learning progress across devices
  • Send account-related notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve our educational content and features
  • Provide customer support when requested

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Authentication and data storage
  • Subject to Google’s Privacy Policy

Apple Sign In (if used)

  • Secure authentication via Apple
  • Subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy

These services may collect certain technical data as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Share your learning data with third parties

We may share information only when:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary to protect our rights
  • You provide explicit consent

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your learning progress
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at iqra.app.support@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect additional personal information from children. Parents can create accounts for their children using their own email address.

10. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside your home country, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: iqra.app.support@gmail.com

We are committed to resolving any concerns about your privacy.
